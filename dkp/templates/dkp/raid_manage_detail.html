Attendees Tab

Check in everyone present at the start of the raid. Unchecked players are treated as Absent.

  • Status updates automatically as you award mob DKP based on each player's kill pattern.
  • You can always override a status manually here after the raid.
  • Partial means they were present for some kills but the pattern doesn't cleanly fit Late or Early Departure.
Mobs Tab

Select which mobs are in scope for this raid, then Save Mobs.

  • Click Award DKP on each mob as it's killed to confirm attendance and distribute DKP.
  • Uncheck anyone not present for that specific kill — a status dropdown appears so you can note why (Late, Left Early, etc.).
  • Use + Add Late Arrival for someone who joined mid-raid.
  • Edit Credits on a greyed-out mob lets you add kill credit without re-awarding DKP — useful if you forgot someone.
  • A yellow Undo notice appears immediately after each award. Navigate away and it's gone.
Loot Tab

Use Quick Award from Drops if the mob is linked to an NPC in the game database — click the mob, click the item, set the recipient and DKP paid.

  • Add Non-Raid Target Loot — for trash drops or global loot. Zero DKP is valid.
  • Open Item for Player Bidding — creates an auction players can bid on through the website.
  • DKP Cost in the Loot Record shows what each winner actually paid.
Status Reference
  • Present — attended every kill
  • Late — missed early kills, stayed to the end
  • Early Departure — present from the start, left before the end
  • Partial — present for some kills, pattern is mixed
  • Absent — did not attend the raid
