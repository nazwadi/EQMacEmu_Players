Bulk Attendance

Check each raid the member attended and hit Save Attendance. Unchecked raids are treated as Not Attended.

  • Checking a raid with no record creates a Present record.
  • Unchecking a raid removes the attendance record entirely.
  • If a member already has a detailed status (Late, Partial, etc.) on a raid, checking it again here won't overwrite that status.
  • To set status, notes, or kill credits, use Edit Raid for that specific raid.
