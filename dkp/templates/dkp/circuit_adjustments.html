Individual Adjustment

Add or subtract DKP for a single member. Use this for corrections, bonuses, or penalties.

  • Enter a positive number to add DKP, a negative number to deduct.
  • A reason is required and will appear in the member's transaction history.
Raid-Wide Adjustment

Applies the same adjustment to every non-absent attendee of the selected raid. Useful for blanket bonuses (e.g., +2 DKP for an unusually long night) or corrections that affect the whole group.

  • Absent members are excluded automatically.
  • A reason is required and appears in each affected member's transaction history.
  • Use Individual Adjustment if only some attendees should be affected.
