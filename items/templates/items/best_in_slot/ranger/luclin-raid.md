## Luclin Raid Gear

---
Gear suggestions for Ranger in the Shadows of Luclin raid endgame, with Velious-era (ToV, Kael) and Plane of Mischief items included where they remain competitive. Primary Luclin sources are Vex Thal and Ssraeshza Temple. Items ordered by score within each slot. Rangers balance melee ATK and ranged archery; 2H swords are the dominant melee option.
