## Luclin Raid Gear

---
Gear suggestions for Shaman in the Shadows of Luclin raid endgame, with Velious-era (ToV, Kael) and Plane of Mischief items included where they remain competitive. Primary Luclin sources are Vex Thal and Ssraeshza Temple. Items ordered by score within each slot. Shamans prioritize WIS and mana for slowing, healing, and buffing; chain armor provides better AC than cloth or leather casters.
