## Planes of Power Raid Gear

---
Gear suggestions for Warrior in the Planes of Power endgame. Primary source is Plane of Time; Quarm drops are the pinnacle. Items ordered by score within each slot. Warriors should stack AC and HP for survivability — PoP gear offers significantly higher AC values than Luclin. Quarm drops dominate most slots.
