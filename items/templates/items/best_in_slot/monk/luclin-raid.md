## Luclin Raid Gear

---
Gear suggestions for Monk in the Shadows of Luclin raid endgame, with Velious-era (ToV, Kael) and Plane of Mischief items included where they remain competitive. Primary Luclin sources are Vex Thal and Ssraeshza Temple. Items ordered by score within each slot. Monks prioritize AC and HP for survivability, and ATK for DPS; leather armor limits AC compared to chain or plate.
