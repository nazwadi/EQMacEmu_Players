## Luclin Raid Gear

---
Gear suggestions for Shadowknight in the Shadows of Luclin raid endgame, with some Velious-era (ToV, Kael) and Plane of Mischief items included where they remain competitive. Primary Luclin sources are Vex Thal and Ssraeshza Temple. Items ordered by score within each slot. Shadowknights value AC and HP for tanking; INT and mana for spellcasting.

*Draft — please correct any slot misassignments or add alternatives you know of.*
