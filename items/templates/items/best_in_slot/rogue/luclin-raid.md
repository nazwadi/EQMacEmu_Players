## Luclin Raid Gear

---
Gear suggestions for Rogue in the Shadows of Luclin raid endgame, with Velious-era (ToV, Kael) and Plane of Mischief items included where they remain competitive. Primary Luclin sources are Vex Thal and Ssraeshza Temple. Items ordered by score within each slot. Rogues maximize ATK and DEX for backstab damage; piercing weapons are required for backstab, though 1HS may score higher due to raw stats.
