## Luclin Raid Gear

---
Gear suggestions for Bard in the Shadows of Luclin raid endgame, with Velious-era (ToV, Kael) and Plane of Mischief items included where they remain competitive. Primary Luclin sources are Vex Thal and Ssraeshza Temple. Items ordered by score within each slot. Bards prioritize AC and HP for survivability while twisting songs; instrument modifiers, DEX, and CHA improve song effectiveness.
